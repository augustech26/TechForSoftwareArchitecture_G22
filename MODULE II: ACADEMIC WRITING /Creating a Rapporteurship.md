Topic: Educational transformation in El Salvador through Artificial Intelligence: Perspectives, challenges and the way forward.

Introduction: El Salvador's education system faces significant challenges, including the digital divide and lack of resources and educational quality. Adopting artificial intelligence (AI) can help personalize learning and improve teaching.

Development: AI can help address the lack of resources and quality of education in El Salvador by personalizing students' learning experiences. It can also close the digital divide by providing access to technology and digital learning tools for students in rural areas. AI can also automate administrative tasks and promote critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Conclusion: In conclusion, educational transformation in El Salvador through artificial intelligence has the potential to address the challenges facing the educational system in the country. However, it is important to consider the ethical and implementation challenges that come with adopting AI in education. Investment in infrastructure and training is necessary to ensure that AI is used effectively and responsibly in El Salvador's education system.
